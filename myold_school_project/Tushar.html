ANU'S ENGLISH ACADEMY

Anu's English Academy is located in UG-1, Nirmal Height, Madia Katra, Raja Mandi, Agra. It is an English Academy established in 1999 by Mr. Anu Sharma (D. K. Sharma) . It has allowance for Education Activities, Teaching Materials; Maintaining Study Centres, Institutes and Classes for Foreign Languages; Training courses, Conducting Exams and Award Degree, Diploma and Other Activities.
