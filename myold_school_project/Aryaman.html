SACHDEVA MILLENIUM SCHOOL


Sachdeva Millennium School, a premier English medium co-educational Institution of Agra is the unit of the DRS Memorial Charitable Trust, founded by a professional lawyer Mr. Rajendra Sachdeva and his wife Mrs. Renu Sachdeva in memory of their heavenly patron Late Shri Dhanpat Rai Sachdeva . Established in the year 2009 with 130 students, SMS Agra today nurtures 1250 students in its campus. A sprawling lush green locale of 2.5 Acres, provides an environment that is conducive to meet the intellectual pursuits and holistic development of all its students who come here to study, to build, to dream, to find their bearing and to blossom into balanced and responsible citizens of the country. It is a path breaker in pursuit of excellence.
