DR. MPS WORLD SCHOOL


Dr. MPS Group started its operations in the year 2004 and today its vast empire is spread in New Delhi, Agra and Mathura. At Dr MPS World School, the children grow and develop in an environment which stimulates a child’s thirst for knowledge, rejuvenates him to embrace the culture and creates passion to enhance the quality of life. To develop a child’s creative self expression, it has well equipped specially designed art studios with facilities for painting, clay modeling, drawing, tie & dye, embroidery, knitting and doll making etc.
