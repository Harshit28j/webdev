NISHANT CLASSES


Established in 2006, Nishant Professional Classes Private Limited offers multidisciplinary learning for aspiring ENGINEERING/MEDICAL/PRE-FOUNDATION students. Over the years, it had became a well known brand in and around Agra as most trusted educational institute. The journey of Nishant Classes started with the vision of imparting best quality education to those students who live the dream of being successful in future. Since last 10 years it is helping in fulfilling the dreams of students and their parents by delivering outstanding results year on year. It is able to do this consistently with the help and support of its experienced and zealous faculty members. Starting with a humble beginning of just 5 students out of which 4 were selected into IITs, provided it the motivation to embark on this journey & endeavour to help more such students realise their dreams by infusing them with the right guidance and direction.
