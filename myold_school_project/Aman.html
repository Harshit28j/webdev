GD GOENKA PUBLIC SCHOOL


GD Goenka Public School has been established to cater to the rising demand for quality education in Agra. The School has a proactive approach to education and its foundations are based on the philosophy that quality education requires consistent efforts and new directions in the area of teaching and learning. It is the endeavour of the school to be student centered in its approach and provide a balanced curriculum that results in the development of the overall personality of the students and to help them in becoming thought leaders of tomorrow. The school has qualified faculty to help in creation of new knowledge which can be translated into classroom teaching. Classrooms are spacious, centrally air conditioned and have lighting arrangements as per international standards. Aesthetically designed furniture has been provided in each classroom, keeping in mind the comfort of the child.
