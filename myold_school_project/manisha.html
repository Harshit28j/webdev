DR. B.R AMBEDKAR UNIVERSITY


The foundation of Dr B.R Ambedkar University, Agra, an institution of 87 years of glorious history and contributing in teaching and research was laid on the 1st of july 1927. It is is located near PaliWal Park in Agra, Uttar pradesh, India. It is formely known as Agra University. It is a state university. The current Vice Chancellor is Arvind Kumar Dixit and Chancellor is Ram Naik . The University and affiliated colleges with university Provide various programs and courses such as Undergraduate Degree Courses , Postgraduate Diploma Courses , Postgraduate Degree Courses , Research / Doctoral Level Courses .
