IIMT COLLEGE OF PARAMEDICAL EDUCATION


IIMT College of Paramedical Education is affiliated to Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty and recognised by Indian Nursing Council Government of India, New Delhi and approved by Government of Uttar Pradesh. The college offers Bachelor of Physiotherapy, Bachelor of Operation theater, Bachelor of Radiology, Bachelor of Optometry, Bachelor of Cardiology, Bachelor of Pathology, Diploma in Physiotherapy, Operation theater, Cardiology, Optometry, Dialysis, C.T. Scan, Emergency and Trama Care courses.
