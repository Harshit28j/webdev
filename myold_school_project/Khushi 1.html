BABU MOHAN LAL ARYA SMARAK ENGINEERING COLLEGE


Babu Mohanlal Arya Smarak Engineering College (BMASEC), Agra is a technical college that comes under the umbrella of Sharda Group of Institutions. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow and accredited by NBA. Sharda Group of Institutions, the founder of BMAS Engineering College, is renowned for its position as North India's largest educational group. In the recent time, SGI got the status of a state university and was rechristened as Sharda University.
