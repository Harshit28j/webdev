SHEMFORD FUTURISTIC SCHOOL

SHEMFORD FUTURISTIC SCHOOL is located in Indrapuram, Adjacent Water Tank, Shamshabad Road, Agra; Affiliated to CBSE. The vision of SHEMFORD Futuristic Schools extends beyond the narrow walls of classroom and textbooks. It believes that education is all about preparing and equipping young people for life and not just for school. With this philosophy as its source of inspiration, it aims to provide a stimulating environment wherein children enjoy learning and can also perform to the best of their abilities, in all areas of our extensive curriculum. By providing inclusive education at SHEMFORD Futuristic Schools, is also aims to build capacity in the children to accept the challenges of life. It believes in inculcating MORAL VALEUS in the children, so as to help them prepare for global challenges. The main stress in SHEMFORD Agra is on improving the communication skills of students, so as to empower them to express their thoughts and feelings in a confident manner
