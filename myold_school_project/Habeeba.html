ANAND ENGINEERING COLLEGE


Anand Engineering College, also known as AEC, is an engineering college in Agra. It is situated at the 18 km milestone on the Agra-Delhi highway (NH-02), spread across 30 acres at a location overlooking Keetham Lake. The college, housed in its own building with 5,00,000 square feet of covered area, was founded in 1998, and the first students graduated in 2002. This college comes under the SGEI (Sharda Group of Euducational Institutes) , a group started in the year 1995, by P.K.Gupta and Y.K.Gupta , after the name of their mother Sharda Devi .
