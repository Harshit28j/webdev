BBA MBA HOTEL MANAGEMENT INSTITUTE


This institte provides DEC-UGC-AICTE CERTIFIED course study with Regular theory and practical classroom sessions under dual certification mode. Special stress is given on communication ability enhancement, co-ordination and decision making skills.
