ADITYA COLLEGE OF LAW


Aditya College of Law was established in Agra by Dr. R.N Gupta , Technical Educational Society in the year 2000. With vision, creative ideas and then thoughts of the founder, the L.L.B and B.A.L.L.B course launched will go a long way. The Institute is deeply concerned and has a particular focus on SC/ST and OBC students for whom the society provides several incentives and facilities. It strives for excellence in what it does, and its graduates are globally acceptable.
