ARYAN INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT AND COMPUTER STUDIES


Aryan Institute of Management & Computer Studies, located in Agra, is managed and run by Pooran Chand Arya Educational and Welfare Society . The institute was set up in 2006. It is affiliated to Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow. Aryan Institute of Management & Computer Studies is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and recognized by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. The college currently conducts postgraduate course in the field of management. The institute offers modern facilities to its students.
