SMT. B.D. JAIN GIRLS DEGREE COLLEGE


Smt. B.D. Jain Girls Degree College is located in Agra and has been considered one of the finest institutions offering post graduation and graduation degree in the state. The institution aims at developing and building students who have integrity, dignity and also confidence to face the various challenges of life. They also aim at developing a feeling of responsibility and also relating to humans around them. They also inculcate in students a sense of responsibility that will guide them in taking decisions in their future without fearing the outcome but putting the necessary thought into it.
