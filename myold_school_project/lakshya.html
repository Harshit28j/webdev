SHARDA GROUP OF INSTITUTION


The Sharda Group of Institution is an educational organisation in Agra. Started in 1996 with its first campus in Farah, Mathura by the name Hindustan College of Science & Technology, the group has grown to have 5 campuses in Agra, and Mathura and one university by the name Sharda University in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh totaling 167 acres of land and 3,500,000 square feet of infrastructure. SGI has 20,000 students and alumni and offers more than 75 programmes in professional education.
